What is Dezinova?

Dezinova is more than just a club; it's a vibrant community for students passionate about computer science, design, and the magic that happens when they collide. We provide a platform to learn, collaborate on projects, and connect with industry professionals.

Workshops

Hands-on sessions on the latest technologies and design tools.

Projects

Collaborate on real-world projects to build your portfolio.

Networking

Connect with peers, alumni, and industry leaders.
